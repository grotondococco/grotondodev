ABOUT ME

I am a developer passionate about building robust, scalable, and efficient architectures with a keen eye for code quality and good software engineering practices. I love working where engineering and design meet, creating solutions that not only work well, but are designed to be robust, maintainable, and perform well.

I currently work as a Software Developer, specializing in microservices development, cross-system integrations, and efficient API design using Java and Spring Boot. I am responsible for building and maintaining server-side applications that power modern web applications and I mainly work on server-side software development having solid experience with REST APIs across various architectures. Throughout my career I have had the opportunity to develop software and provide consulting in different contexts - I have taken part in projects for the public administration, Italian ministries, and banks as part of medium- and large-sized companies. This has allowed me to face various technical challenges and increase my experience in integrating complex systems and collaborating with distributed teams (I speak fluent front-endish)

In my spare time I play guitar, relax by organizing barbecues with friends and family, and always find time to cultivate new passions related to the world of IT.
